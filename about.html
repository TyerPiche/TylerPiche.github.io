Our Mission

Harvest Hope exists to reduce hunger and improve wellbeing by providing dignified access to nutritious food and community resources.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by neighbors and volunteers, Harvest Hope started as a small weekend food box program and has grown to serve hundreds of households annually.

How you can get involved

  1. Volunteer at the pantry (packing, distribution).
  2. Donate non-perishable or monetary gifts.
  3. Share our mission on social media and with neighbors.
